21 Points

A simple Intereactive Game Realised by JavaScript


by Julianna Wu



How to play the game?

  • The gole of this game is to collect cards that add up to approach 21 points as close as you can to beat the opponent.
  • Each round, you and computer could choose to "Go on"or "End it". If "Go on", you'd receive a new card; if "End it" , computer would generate the current sum of you two and declear the winder.
  • If your cards add up bigger than 21, you'd be exlopded!
  • You can have at most 6 cards before you exploded.
  • So do the computer, but you won't know whether computer has exploded or not till none of you want to "Go on" anymore.
  • But once you exploded, you can't call a newcard anymore and computer would generate the result
